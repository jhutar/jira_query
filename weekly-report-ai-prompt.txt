# AI Weekly Report Summary Prompt

Use this prompt with an LLM (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) along with the output of `./ai_weekly_report.py` to generate a polished weekly status update.

---

**Context:** Attached is a raw weekly status report for the past 7 days, generated from Jira issues and linked Pull Request descriptions across several engineering teams (Konflux, Pipelines, ConsoleDot, and Satellite).

**Task:** Please synthesize this data into a professional, concise weekly summary.

**Requirements:**
1. **Weekly Overview:** Start with a 2-3 sentence overview of the most significant progress made across all teams this week.
2. **Team Highlights:** For each team (Konflux, Pipelines, ConsoleDot, and Satellite), provide 2-3 bullet points summarizing their key activities. Focus on *outcomes* and *impact* (what was finished, what moved into review) rather than just listing ticket IDs. Use the PR descriptions to provide technical context where helpful.
3. **Weekly Momentum:** Briefly highlight the transition of issues from "In Progress" or "New" to "In Review" or "Finished". Identify any blockers if they are apparent from the descriptions.
4. **Looking Ahead:** If the data suggests upcoming milestones or themes for the next week, mention them briefly.
5. **Tone:** Professional, direct, and focused on progress. Avoid technical jargon where a simpler explanation of the benefit exists.
